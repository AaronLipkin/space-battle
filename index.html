Welcome to your first space battle. You are the captain of the USS Starkiller. Your command post is currently hidden over to the right, you can press "command - option - J". You have two warp corridors you could go down. Warp corridor one will take you into Klingon territory. Klingon ships are powerful, but unrefined. They have strong shields and stronger weapons but their targeting systems are ancient. Down the other corridor is Romulan space. Romulan ships are relatively fragile and their lasers won't exactly rip through your hull, but they are millimeter precise on their aiming. Lucky for you, the USS Starkiller is a beast, with guns on par with the Klingon's and targeting systems that even a Romulan would envy. Your task is to blast your way through 8 enemy ships without getting blown up yourself. To help you on your way, about a third of enemies will leave behind materials you can use to repair some damage to your hull. Good luck, and don't die!

So who's space would you like to invade?
